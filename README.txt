All setup & usage instruction are now on the Wiki:
 https://github.com/demianbadin/Ghettostation/wiki

Simplified and updated version of the original Ghettostation from https://github.com/KipK/Ghettostation
Based on Arduino Mega2560

Reads data from UAVTalk, MAVLink, Multiwii MSP, NMEA GPS, Ublox GPS, and Frsky SmartPort.   
 
Credits:
Authors: Noños FPV Team! (Demian Badin, Daniel De Bonis, Leonardo Cabrera)

Sample codes & inspirations from different projects:
- ghettostation: (https://github.com/KipK/Ghettostation)
- multiwii (https://github.com/multiwii/multiwii-firmware)
- minimosd-extra (http://code.google.com/p/minimosd-extra/)
- UAVTalk implementation from minoposd (http://code.google.com/p/minoposd/)
- NMEA & Ublox libraries from Jordi Muñoz and Jose Julio (DIYDrones.com)
